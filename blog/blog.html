About Me:

I live in Upper Hutt with my wife and two young children and am currently studying at Enspiral Dev Academy after spending 17 years in the media.

The background image I chose for this page is a painting by one of my favourite artists, Henri Rousseau. What I especially love is how it illustrates momentum. Everything in the painting seems to be drawing your eye towards something.

The imagery at work here seems especially relevant to me at the moment. The tiger is the very embodiment of strength, yet it's face seems to communicate a sense of fear, anticipation and almost panic.

Whenever I've attempted something difficult in life, I've always felt a mixture of courage and trepidation when facing it. It's with these traits in mind that I'm undertaking my studies at Dev Academy.
